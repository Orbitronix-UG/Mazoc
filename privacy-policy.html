Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2025

Mazoc ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

Mazoc is designed with privacy in mind. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal information such as:

  • Names, email addresses, or phone numbers
  • Financial account information or bank details
  • Personal identification documents
  • Location data or GPS coordinates

  • Social media accounts or profiles
  • Contact lists or address books

1.2 Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage statistics to improve our app functionality, including:

  • App performance metrics and crash reports
  • Feature usage patterns and popular calculators
  • Error logs and debugging information
  • Device information (model, operating system version, screen resolution)
  • App version and installation date
  • Session duration and interaction patterns
  • Language and regional settings

1.3 What We Don't Collect

Importantly, we do not collect:

  • Your calculation inputs, formulas, or results
  • Financial data you enter (loan amounts, interest rates, etc.)
  • Personal preferences or saved settings
  • Any text or numbers you type in the app
  • Photos, videos, or files from your device
  • Browsing history or web activity

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used solely for:

  • Improving app performance and user experience
  • Fixing bugs and technical issues
  • Adding new features and functionality
  • Ensuring app stability and reliability
  • Optimizing app performance for different devices
  • Understanding which features are most popular
  • Providing better customer support
  • Ensuring compatibility with new operating system versions

3. Data Storage and Security

3.1 Local Storage

All calculations and data entered in Mazoc are stored locally on your device. We do not have access to:

  • Your calculation inputs or results
  • Your saved preferences or settings
  • Any financial data you enter
  • Your calculation history
  • Custom formulas or saved calculations

When you delete the app, all your data is permanently removed from your device.

3.2 Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect any data we may collect, including:

  • Encryption of data in transit using HTTPS
  • Secure data storage practices
  • Regular security updates and patches
  • Access controls and authentication
  • Regular security audits
  • Compliance with industry security standards

3.3 Data Retention

For any anonymous usage data we may collect:

  • We retain it for a maximum of 2 years
  • You can request deletion of this data at any time
  • Data is automatically deleted after the retention period
  • We do not sell or share this data with third parties
